Hello, Students!

Ever wanted to connect with other college students around the world taking the same class as you?

Use PostNote to share your notes from class, ask questions to other students, and create a collaborative learning environment.

Get Started!
Post

You can use PostNote to keep your notes from class all in one place. Write your notes from your profile or even upload your notes to keep them all in one place.

Create

With PostNote you can create your own flashcards from your notes for an efficient studying experience.

Connect

Use PostNote to connect with college students around the world, with chat and commenting features. You can ask collaborate with students from different backgrounds and experiences.
